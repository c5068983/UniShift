UniShift is a smart shift management system that helps users plan, track, and manage work schedules efficiently with ease and clarity.

Why UniShift?

Many students and part-time workers struggle to find flexible jobs and manage shifts efficiently. UniShift solves this by bringing everything into one simple platform.

Our Core Principles

⚡ Simplicity

Easy-to-use interface for managing shifts without complexity.

🚀 Speed

Fast shift applications and instant updates.

🔍 Transparency

Clear status tracking for all shift requests.

🔄 Flexibility

Users can manage and cancel shifts anytime.

Key Features

⚙️ Shift Management

Create, edit, and manage shifts in a simple and efficient way.

🔔 Real-time Updates

Instant notifications for shift approvals, cancellations, and changes.

🔐 Secure System

OTP-based authentication ensures secure access for all users.

📊 Smart Tracking

Track shift history, status, and performance easily.

⏱ Flexible Scheduling

Users can apply, cancel, or update shifts anytime.
